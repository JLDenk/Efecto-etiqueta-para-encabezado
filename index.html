Rules & Settings

Seeding

Based on rating points. Inheriting the seed of a defeated higher seeded player.

Civilizations

Drafted for each series, no repeat.

Maps

Drafted, the map for G1 is always the remaining map

Restarts

Only in case of bugged maps (to be called by players before 2 minutes in-game time). Restart conditions: defined in the handbook.

Player colours

Blue (higher seeded player) vs yellow or, if needed visually, red (lower seeded player).

Laming

Allowed.
